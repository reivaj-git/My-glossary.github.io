practice 57

appealing

adjective

definition: attractive or interesting.

example: He had a nice smile and an appealing personality.

childish

adjective

definition: silly, like the behaviour of a child.

example: Don’t be so childish!

christen

verb

definition: to give a baby a name at a Christian ceremony and make them a member of the Christian Church.

example: to officially give a name in a Christian ceremony.

pervasive

adjective

definition: present or noticeable in every part of a thing or place.

example: The influence of Freud is pervasive in her books.

resourceful

adjective

definition: skilled at solving problems and making decisions on your own.

example: She’s a very resourceful manager.

scamper

verb

definition: to run quickly and with small steps, like a child or a small animal

example: I shouted and the dog scampered off.

supposedly

adverb

definition: used to show that you do not believe that something you have been told is true

example: Well, the tickets are supposedly in the mail.

tame

verb

definition: to make a wild animal tame

example: the way they used to tame wild horses
