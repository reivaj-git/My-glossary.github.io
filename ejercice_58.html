disease

noun

definition: (an) illness of people, animals, plants, etc., caused by infection or a failure of health rather than by an accident.

example: They reported a sudden outbreak of the disease in the south of the country.

encouraged

adjective

definition: having more confidence or hope about something

example: She felt encouraged by their promise of support.

fur

example: She stroked the rabbit's soft fur.
