alloy

noun

definition: a metal that is made by mixing two or more metals, or a metal and another substance.

example: Brass is an alloy of copper and zinc.

carve

verb

definition: to make something by cutting into especially wood or stone, or to cut into the surface of stone, wood, etc.

example: This totem pole is carved from/out of a single tree trunk.

clay

noun

definition: thick, heavy soil that is soft when wet, and hard when dry or baked, used for making bricks and containers.

example: Clay is used for making bricks and pots.

clerk

noun

definition: a person who works in an office, dealing with records or performing general office duties.

example: The clerk checked us in and gave us our key.

knowledge

noun

definition: understanding of or information about a subject that you get by experience or study, either known by one person or by people generally

example:Her knowledge of English grammar is very extensive

Molten

adjective

definition: Molten metal or rock is in a liquid state because of great heat

example:Condensation of molten material around the ablation site is unfavorable for producing fine and debris-free microstructures in materials processing.

strip

verb

definition:to remove, pull, or tear the covering or outer layer from something.

example:Because of the pollution, the trees are almost completely stripped of bark.
