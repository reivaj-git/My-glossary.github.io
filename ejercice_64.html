practice 64

chain

noun

definition: His resignation was followed by a remarkable chain of events.

example: She has built up a chain of 180 bookshops across the country.

leisure

noun

definition: the time when you are not working or doing other duties.

example: The town lacks leisure facilities such as a swimming pool or squash courts.

likewise

adverb

definition: in the same way.

example: Just water these plants twice a week, and likewise the ones in the bedroom.

path

noun

definition: a route or track between one place and another, or the direction in which something is moving.

example: It will be several days before snowploughs clear a path (through) to the village.

ubiquitous

adjective

definition: seeming to be everywhere.

example: Leather is very much in fashion this season, as is the ubiquitous denim.
