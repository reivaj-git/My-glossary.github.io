Pitch

Noun

definition: the level or degree of something.

example: The piano and organ were tuned to the same pitch (= note).

pluck

Verb

definition: to pull something, especially with a sudden movement, in order to remove it.

example: Caged birds sometimes pluck out their breast feathers.

raise

verb

definition: to lift something to a higher position.

example: Would all those in favour please raise their hands?

sharp

adjective

definition: having a thin edge or point that can cut something or make a hole in something,producing or describing a quick, strong pain that makes you feel like you have been cut.

example: a knife with a sharp edge/blade.

string

Noun

definition: (a piece of) strong, thin rope made by twisting very thin threads together, used for fastening and tying things.

example: When you pull the strings, the puppet's arms and legs move.
