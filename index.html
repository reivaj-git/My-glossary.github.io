hang

verb

definition: to fasten something so that the top part is fixed but the lower part is free to move, or to be held in this way.

definition: not having something, or not having enough of something.

spread

definition: to arrange something so that it covers a large area,to affect a larger number of people,to move a soft substance across a surface so that it covers it.
